About

"Snipe the block" is a very simple toy contract deployed on the NEAR testnet. Your objective is to guess the block that your transaction ("shot") is executed on.

The block started running up the hill the moment it saw you stumbling upon that page, the contract will give you feedback on your shots. Bulleye the block and you will be rewarded ONE testnet NEAR!
